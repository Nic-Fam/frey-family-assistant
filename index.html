Frey Family Assistant

The Frey Family Assistant is a private household-management tool operated by Nic Frey (sole proprietor). It sends SMS notifications from +1 (877) 768-0783 to the two members of the Frey household, Nic Frey and Shelli Frey, who operate the service.

Who receives messages

Messages are sent only to the two household members who operate this service. There are no external recipients.

Message types

  • Daily morning schedule briefings (weather and the day's calendar)
  • Weekly schedule previews
  • Calendar event reminders
  • Leave-by-time alerts based on calendar and traffic
  • Package delivery and tracking alerts
  • Confirmation previews for actions the household members request
  • System notifications such as errors and renewals

How recipients opt in

The two recipients are the operators of the service. They enrolled their own mobile phone numbers to receive these notifications when they set up the service. No third parties receive messages.

Message frequency

Varies by day; typically fewer than 100 messages per month per recipient. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt out

Reply STOP to any message to stop receiving messages at any time. Reply HELP for help. Every message ends with: "Frey Family Assistant. Reply STOP to stop."

Privacy

Recipient phone numbers are used solely to deliver these household notifications. They are never shared or sold.
